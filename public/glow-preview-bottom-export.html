Deterministic, no-LLM. Each card is the same template fed a different config — only the 4 palette colours, the 2 fonts, the surface (dark/light), the headline and the name change. Resize to see container-query scaling. The AirPods are embedded in this file.

Surface → mode

surface luminance < 0.42 ⇒ dark (black field, white text); else light (white field, black text).

Bloom

The 2 most-saturated of primary/secondary/tertiary/accent → inner→outer bloom hue. Neutrals skipped.

Accent word

accent colours the last words of the headline. Use a chromatic accent so it reads on black & white.

Fonts

titleFont = headline, bodyFont = sub. Google fonts auto-load; system fonts as-is.
